Frequently Asked Questions

How long until we see results?

▼
Infrastructure goes live 1 week after signup and message volume scales up over the first month. First replies start coming in week 2. Expect booked demos as early as end of week 2, usually by week 3-4. Pipeline compounds from there.

What if the meetings aren't qualified?

▼
You define the ICP. We use a 5-tool enrichment waterfall with deep scoring and segmentation to make sure every prospect matches your criteria. On the call, we'll show you exactly how this targeting works with real data.

We've tried outbound before — how is this different?

▼
Most outbound fails because of bad data, generic messaging, or no infrastructure. We own the full stack: dedicated sending domains with proper DNS, inbox warming, AI-powered prospect research, and multi-channel execution across email, LinkedIn, and phone. It's a system, not a spray-and-pray.

Our market is too niche — can you find our buyers?

▼
That's actually where we excel. Our multi-source data waterfall crosses 5 separate databases to find verified contacts in even the most specialized industries. On the call, we'll show you live research for your specific market.

How much of my time will this take?

▼
This is fully done-for-you. You'll spend about 2 hours in the first week on ICP definition, then receive weekly Slack updates. We handle everything else — infrastructure, list building, messaging, sending, and response handling.

What's included in the price?

▼
Full infrastructure (dedicated domains, DNS, inbox warming), AI deep-research on every prospect, multi-draft messaging, multi-channel execution (email + LinkedIn + phone), real-time response handling, and weekly performance reporting. No hidden fees, no per-lead charges.

Can we start with a pilot?

▼
Our minimum engagement is structured around the time it takes to see real results — typically 3-6 months. Outbound is a compound system; cutting it short before the data compounds means you won't see the full picture. We'll walk through the ROI math on the call so you can see exactly what to expect.

How many meetings should we expect?

▼
It depends on your market, volume, and close rate — which is why we use a live ROI calculator on the call. With conservative benchmarks (2% reply rate, 5% positive, 70% show rate), most clients see 5-15 qualified meetings per month from email alone.

What do I need to provide to get started?

▼
Just your time for a 1-hour ICP definition session in week 1. We'll need access to your brand assets (logo, colors) for domain setup, and any existing case studies or customer stories. Everything else — data, infrastructure, messaging — we build from scratch.

What happens after onboarding?

▼
Campaigns go live in month 2. You'll receive weekly performance reports via Slack, real-time positive reply notifications, and monthly optimization reviews. We continuously refine targeting, messaging, and channel mix based on live data.
